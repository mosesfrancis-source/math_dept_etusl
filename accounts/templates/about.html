About ETUSL

Eastern Technical University of Sierra Leone (ETUSL) is committed to empowering the next generation of African innovators and leaders through cutting-edge technical education and research. Located at Combema Road, Kenema, Sierra Leone, the university operates with a strategic plan spanning 2023–2027.

With over 5,000 enrolled students, 200+ faculty members, and 50+ technical programs across nine faculties, ETUSL stands as one of Sierra Leone's leading technical institutions with an impressive 85% graduate employment rate.

Department of Mathematics

The Department of Mathematics sits within the Faculty of Basic & Applied Science and offers rigorous programs in both pure and applied mathematics. Our department prepares students for careers in research, education, finance, data science, engineering, and public service.

We are committed to academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and producing graduates who contribute meaningfully to national development in Sierra Leone and beyond.
