2025 — Admissions
Phase One Interview Sessions Underway — 2025/2026
ETUSL is currently conducting Phase One interview sessions for students who applied for the 2025/2026 academic year. Pending applicants are encouraged to check their application status via the admissions portal at app.etusl.edu.sl.
2025 — New Programs
Post-Graduate Master's Programs Launched
ETUSL has successfully introduced new Post-Graduate Master's programs in Education, Nursing, and Public Health through the School of Post-Graduate Studies. Applications are open for the 2025/2026 academic year.
2025 — Campus Development
Bunumbu Campus Expansion in Progress
Construction work is well underway at the Bunumbu Campus in Kailahun District. The expansion includes new hostel facilities, lecture halls, and staff quarters — significantly increasing the university's capacity to accommodate more students.
2025 — New Program
BSc Medical Laboratory Science Program Launched
ETUSL has successfully launched the Bachelor of Science in Medical Laboratory Science program under the Faculty of Health & Disaster Management. This new program aims to address the country's healthcare workforce needs.
2025 — Portal
Mathematics Department Student Portal Now Live
The Department of Mathematics has launched its online student portal, enabling students to register for courses, view grades, track GPA, and access department resources from anywhere. Contact the registrar for your Registration PIN.
