Getting Started

All new students must complete registration through the ETU Mathematics Portal. Registration requires a PIN issued by the department admin — check your email for your PIN before attempting to register.

  1. Receive your Registration PIN from the department admin via email.
  2. Visit the portal and click Student Registration.
  3. Enter your Student ID, personal details, and PIN.
  4. Set a password and log in to access your dashboard.
  5. Register for your courses through My Registration.
